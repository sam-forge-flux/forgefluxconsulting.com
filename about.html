Our Story

Built for Impact, Not Just Audits.

Forge & Flux Consulting was founded at the intersection of two disciplines that rarely talk to each other — because the intersection is exactly where the problems live.

The Origin

Where Quality Meets Technical Development

Most consulting firms specialize. They're either quality consultants or technical development advisors — and the two worlds rarely overlap. That separation is precisely the problem.

We've watched organizations build ISO 9001 quality systems that get certified and then quietly abandoned. We've seen Stage-Gate frameworks installed by well-meaning consultants that became bureaucratic tollbooths — slowing the engineers they were supposed to support. We've watched "stars" get buried in documentation overhead while their actual innovation sat idle.

The pattern in every case was the same: the system was designed around the methodology, not the organization. And it was almost always built on top of existing tools rather than inside them — creating parallel workflows that people resisted from day one.

"The certificate gets framed. The system gets ignored. The innovation process becomes compliance theater. We built Forge & Flux because organizations deserve better than that."

Forge & Flux was created to solve both problems from a single, integrated perspective. Quality and technical development are not separate disciplines — they're two sides of the same operational coin. An organization that can't manage quality can't scale innovation. An organization that can't innovate can't sustain quality. The systems need to be integrated, not parallel.

Why We Exist

Three Problems We Were Built to Solve

The M365 Advantage

Organizations pay for Microsoft 365 and then buy separate quality platforms, project management tools, and documentation systems on top of it. We recognized that M365 — properly architected — is already a complete operational platform. It just needs the right structure and the right guidance to reach its potential.

Everything we build is native. No new licenses. No migration headaches. No parallel platforms to maintain.

Guidance Over Binders

The consulting industry has a documentation addiction. When the engagement ends, clients are left with binders, procedures, and templates — but no organizational understanding of why the system works. The system gets used for the first audit and then quietly shelved.

We deliver understanding, not just documents. Every engagement includes deliberate training on the logic behind the system so that your team can own it, adapt it, and use it for real work.

Impactful Consulting Beyond the Certificate

ISO 9001 certification is a milestone, not a destination. Stage-Gate implementation is infrastructure, not an end state. We measure success by what happens after the engagement closes — are quality metrics improving? Are projects moving faster? Are your people using the system by choice, not obligation?

That's what impactful consulting looks like. That's what we're here to deliver.

Our Mission

Systems That Drive Value

We exist to build operational systems that make organizations better at what they actually do — not just more compliant, not just more documented, but more effective, more agile, and more confident in their own capabilities.

  • Build systems that outlast the engagement — designed to be owned and evolved by the client
  • Use the tools organizations already have — no new platforms, no added complexity
  • Teach the logic, not just the process — so your team understands the "WHY" behind every element
  • Integrate quality and innovation — because operational excellence requires both
  • Measure success by organizational performance, not just certification status

How We Work

The Forge & Flux Approach

We Start With Your Reality

Every engagement begins with a clear-eyed assessment of your current state — not a generic questionnaire, but a structured analysis of how your organization actually operates. We map the gaps between where you are and where the standard or framework requires you to be, and we design from there.

We Build Inside Your Environment

Every system we build is native to Microsoft 365. We use SharePoint for document control and project governance, Teams for collaboration and process channels, Power Automate for workflow logic, and Planner or Project for portfolio visibility. Your team doesn't change how they work — the platform works around them.

We Train for Understanding

Adoption is the hardest part of any systems implementation — and it almost always fails when people don't understand why the system works the way it does. We build training into every engagement, focused on the logic behind each component so your team can use, maintain, and evolve the system independently.

We Stay Engaged

The engagement doesn't end at deployment. We support your team through first audits, first gate reviews, and the inevitable "what do we do with this edge case" moments that come with any new system. We're your strategic partner, not just your implementation vendor.
