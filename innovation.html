Technical Development Logic

Stage-Gate & InnOps Frameworks That Deliver ROI.

Standardized innovation infrastructure that empowers your best technical talent — without the weight of micromanagement or the overhead of new software platforms.

The Innovation Bottleneck

Most technical development processes fail in one of three ways:

  • Stage-gate systems become bureaucratic tollbooths that slow projects without adding clarity
  • Teams execute the process without understanding the "WHY" — so they game the gates instead of using them
  • "Star" engineers get buried in documentation overhead instead of doing the work they were hired to do

The result: projects stall, ROI is unrealized, and your most talented people quietly disengage.

The InnOps Approach

We build a Stage-Gate "scaffold" directly inside Microsoft 365 — lightweight enough to move fast, structured enough to protect your investment:

  • Gate criteria tied to real business outcomes, not checkbox compliance
  • Teams learn the logic behind the framework — not just the forms to fill out
  • Project velocity tracked automatically in M365, not in disconnected spreadsheets
  • Technical leadership layer that guides without micromanaging

Your innovators get the structure they need to move confidently — and the autonomy they need to do great work.

The Innovation Track

From Framework to Velocity

Three deliberate phases. Designed to embed logic, not just process.

01

Current-State Audit (The Forge)

We evaluate your current technical development process — how projects are initiated, governed, resourced, and measured. We identify friction points, invisible bottlenecks, and misaligned incentives. The result is a clear picture of why projects stall or underperform, and a design brief for the framework that will replace it.

Deliverable: Process Assessment Report with Stage-Gate design brief

02

Native Framework Build (The System)

We build your Stage-Gate framework inside Microsoft 365 — project intake forms, gate review templates, pipeline tracking dashboards, resource logs, and decision records. The system is designed around how your specific teams work, not a generic methodology. Your people don't change platforms; the platform changes to fit the process.

Deliverable: Deployed M365-native Stage-Gate system with all templates and tracking tools

03

Strategic Activation (The Flux)

We train your technical leaders and project teams on the logic behind the framework — not just how to use it, but why each gate exists and what decisions it's designed to protect. Deliverable: Team training sessions, leadership advisory, quarterly framework reviews
